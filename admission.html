Admissions

Thank you for your interest in our university. We offer a wide range of programs and opportunities for students to succeed in their academic and personal goals. We look forward to reviewing your application and welcoming you to our community.

Application Process

To apply to our university, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete and submit the online application form.
  2. Pay the application fee.
  3. Submit official transcripts from all previous institutions attended.
  4. Submit test scores (SAT, ACT, GRE, etc.) if required for your program.
  5. Submit any additional application materials required by your program.

Application Deadlines

The application deadlines for our programs are as follows:

  • Fall semester: August 1st
  • Spring semester: December 1st
  • Summer semester: April 1st

Please note that some programs may have different deadlines or may only accept applications for certain semesters. Please check with your program for specific deadlines and requirements.

Tuition and Financial Aid

Our tuition rates vary by program and residency status. Please visit our Tuition and Fees page for more information.

We offer a variety of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study programs. Please visit our Financial Aid page for more information and to apply.
